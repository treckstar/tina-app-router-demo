At the time of posting this article, Kapsalon Lieve has just launched its
website. Having a website helps people find your contact information.

![Kapsalon Lieve screenshot](http://res.cloudinary.com/darenmalfait/image/upload/v1673087228/kapsalon-lieve-screenshot_fls44c.png "Kapsalon Lieve screenshot")

You can see the website at
[https://www.kapsalonlieve.be](https://www.kapsalonlieve.be).

Under the hood, the website is built with NextJS and a git-based headless CMS:
Tina.

![kapsalon lieve lighthouse
scores](https://res.cloudinary.com/darenmalfait/image/upload/v1668711486/kapsalonlieve-lighthouse.png "kapsalon lieve lighthouse scores")

Currently, the website is a one-pager mainly for discoverability on the
internet. However, the website can quickly scale with the CMS providing the
data.
